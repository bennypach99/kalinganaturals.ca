Our Story

Every bar begins in the Philippines.

It starts with a partner managing a small workshop, farmers who grow calamansi and moringa down the road, and a promise: pay people what their work is worth. Each bar is mixed, poured, cut and cured by hand — then carried across the ocean to Canada.

We're a family-run social enterprise rooted in bayanihan — community, lifting one another. When you buy a bar, a family back home is part of the reason it exists.
